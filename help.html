Help & FAQ

Everything you need to create stunning iPhone case designs.

🚀 Quick Start

  1. Open the Editor
  2. Select your iPhone model from the top toolbar
  3. Choose a template or start from scratch
  4. Upload your image, add text, or pick shapes/stickers
  5. Use the Properties panel (right) to adjust colors, sizes, effects
  6. Click 💾 Save to store locally, or 📤 Export to download

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is CaseCraft really free?
Yes! CaseCraft is 100% free. There are no payments, no subscriptions, no checkout, and no accounts. It's a design tool you can use freely, forever.
Does my data get uploaded to a server?
No. All your designs are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. Your work stays on your device completely private.
How do I export for printing?
Click the 📤 Export button in the toolbar. Choose PNG format and select 300 DPI for professional print quality. The file will be downloaded directly to your computer.
What image formats can I upload?
You can upload PNG, JPG/JPEG, and WebP images. For best results, use PNG with transparent backgrounds for designs you want to layer over gradients.
How do I resize and rotate an image layer?
Click on an image layer to select it. Drag it to move it. Use Ctrl+Scroll to resize it. Drag the orange ⬤ handle above the selection box to rotate it. You can also enter exact values in the Properties panel on the right.
Can I save and reopen my work?
Yes! Click 💾 Save to save to your browser's local storage. You can reload the page and click "Restore autosaved work" to continue. You can also export your project as a JSON file and import it later.
What are the iPhone 17 measurements?
iPhone 17 series dimensions are estimated placeholders as the phone wasn't released at the time of writing. The app shows a warning banner when you select these models. You can edit the preset dimensions yourself via the ⚙️ button next to the model selector.
Does it work offline?
Yes! After you've loaded the page once, CaseCraft works fully offline. The only external resources are Google Fonts (for the UI typography) — if offline, system fonts are used as fallback.
How do I add a glow effect to text?
Select a text layer, then in the Properties panel on the right, scroll down to "Glow Effect". Check "Enable Glow", choose a glow color, and adjust the blur radius. Neon glows work best on dark backgrounds.
Can I use CaseCraft on mobile?
Yes! The editor is mobile-friendly. Touch drag to move layers, pinch to resize. The left panel collapses on small screens. For the best experience, use a tablet or desktop.
