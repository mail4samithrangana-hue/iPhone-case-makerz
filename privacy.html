Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

No Data Collection

CaseCraft does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. This is a static web application that runs entirely within your browser.

Local Storage

CaseCraft uses your browser's localStorage to save your design projects, templates, and settings. This data:

  • Lives only on your device
  • Is never sent to any server
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing browser storage

Fonts & CDN

The app loads Google Fonts from the Google CDN (fonts.googleapis.com). This may cause Google to receive your IP address and browser information as a normal part of loading those fonts. No other external requests are made.

Images You Upload

Images you upload into the editor exist only in browser memory and localStorage. They are never sent anywhere.

No Analytics

CaseCraft includes no analytics, tracking scripts, cookies, or advertising.

Contact

For questions, open an issue on the GitHub repository where this app is hosted.
